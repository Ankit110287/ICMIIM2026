About the Conference

A Premier Forum on Materials & AI Modelling

The International Conference on Material Insights and Intelligent Modelling (ICMIIM 2026) is a premier interdisciplinary gathering bringing together researchers, engineers, and industry experts working at the intersection of materials science, computational modelling, and artificial intelligence.

Hosted by [Your University Name], ICMIIM 2026 provides a vibrant platform for exchanging breakthrough ideas in advanced materials characterisation, data-driven discovery, and intelligent simulation techniques.

This year's theme, "Bridging Material Science and Machine Intelligence", encourages contributions spanning experimental materials research, AI/ML-based modelling, multiscale simulations, and smart manufacturing.

All accepted papers will be published in conference proceedings with an ISBN and considered for indexing in leading databases including Scopus and Web of Science.
